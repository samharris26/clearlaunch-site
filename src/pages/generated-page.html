Answers to common questions.

Not seeing what you need? Reach out and we’ll help you understand if ClearLaunch fits your next launch.

How does ClearLaunch decide when to post?

ClearLaunch uses a mix of public engagement benchmarks, platform‑specific best practices, and your launch window to propose posting times. You can also override any suggestion manually—your plan updates automatically.

Do I need to connect my social media accounts?

No. ClearLaunch can be used purely as a planning tool. Connecting platforms lets you send scheduled posts or track performance more easily, but it’s optional.

Can ClearLaunch help if I only use one main platform?

Yes. Many solo founders run launches on just one channel. ClearLaunch still creates a full sequence of teasers, launch posts, and follow‑ups tailored to that platform.

What if my launch date moves?

Adjust your launch date and ClearLaunch will shift your entire plan—pre‑launch, launch day, and post‑launch follow‑ups—while keeping the structure intact.
